Cooking

Cooking is something that I grew up doing with my mom and my grandma. I am so excited that I now have a daughter I can pass the cooking tradition on to. On this page you will find recipes for the week, and how my toddler enjoys each meal so you could try it with your toddler as well.

This week, I made one of my husbands favorite chicken recipes, Mississippi Chicken! I know that this chicken doesn't look like much in the first photo, but I promise this is the BEST chicken! You need the following ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • 1 Ranch Dressing Packet
  • 1 Au Jus Gravy Mix
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Banana Peppers
  • Butter
  • Cheese Slices. I prefer Colby Jack or Pepper Jack!

    • You will season your chicken with salt & pepper,the ranch dressing, and the gravy mix. Then place Banana Peppers on top and cute thin slices of butter on top. I bake mine at 450 for about 35-45 minutes depending on the size of the chicken breast. Once cooked, top the chicken with the cheese slices and place back in the oven until melted. I normally serve with rice. Such a yummy meal!
